IMAGINEERING THE FUTURE: IMAGINEERS EXPO25

Where Imagination Meets Engineering

About the Event

The Imagineering Expo is a celebration of creative engineering and innovation in experiential design. This event explores how engineers, storytellers, and designers come together to build immersive, interactive experiences that inspire wonder and bring ideas to life. From robotics and ride systems to themed environments and emerging technologies, the expo showcases how imagination and technical skill can create powerful connections with people of all ages. Attendees will discover how fields like mechanical engineering, computer science, architecture, marketing, and media can work together to shape the future of entertainment, education, and community impact. Whether you're a future engineer, creative thinker, or storyteller, the Imagineering Expo invites you to explore what happens when you combine innovation with imagination — and design with heart.

What to Expect

Where Ideas Take Center Stage.

  • Hands-on Demos: Experience interactive demonstrations from engineering projects that blend creativity and upcoming technology.
  • Inspiring Imagineer Spotlights: Hear personal stories and career insights from industry professionals who are designing the future of immersive entertainment, innovation, and themed environments.
  • Collaborative Design Challenges: Join fellow attendees in fun, fast-paced hallenges where you can let you imagination and problem-solving skill go wild and reimagine the future!
